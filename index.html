Glow Sprint is a free hyper-casual browser game. Race down a neon city expressway, dodging glowing traffic across three lanes. Build streaks, trigger Overdrive, and chase your high score — no download required.

How to Play

  • Tap / click anywhere (or press Space / Enter) to shift lanes: Left → Mid → Right → Left.
  • Dodge oncoming traffic cars to score points. The longer you survive, the faster it gets.
  • Pass close to a car's side for a Near-Miss bonus (+3) and faster multiplier growth.
  • Fill the PHASE bar with clean dodges to trigger Overdrive — speed up, earn more, look cool.
  • Touch a car = game over. Tap again to restart instantly.

FAQ

Does Glow Sprint work on mobile?

Yes — tap anywhere on screen to change lanes. The canvas scales to fit your phone without any app install.

Is there a save system?

Your all-time best score is saved locally in your browser via localStorage. It persists between sessions on the same device.

What is Overdrive?

Overdrive activates automatically when the PHASE bar is full. For a few seconds the world speeds up, colors shift, and your score multiplier climbs faster. Survive it for a massive point haul.

Can I share my score?

Yes — after a game over, tap the Share button to post your score. On supported devices it uses the native share sheet; otherwise it copies a challenge link to your clipboard.
