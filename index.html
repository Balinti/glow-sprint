About / How to Play

Glow Sprint is a hyper-casual HTML5 tap timing game. Your neon orb races through a shifting neon city. Tap or press Space/Enter to cycle through three color phases: Cyan → Magenta → Yellow. Match your phase to the incoming gate's color to pass through safely.

Perfect: Tap to match just as a gate closes in for bonus points and streak. Build your streak to unlock a multiplier up to ×8.

Overcharge Save: Reach a 12-streak to bank one safety save that absorbs a wrong-color crash.

Prism Gates (after 90s) are always safe — pass for bonus points regardless of phase.

Works on desktop and mobile browsers. Free, no download required.
